About LMS

A web-based Leave Management System (LMS) is a Human Resource (HR) software web application that is designed with the goal of managing human capital leave request, procedures and records. It is captured to be a useful HR subsidiary system segregated for managing employee leave matters.

About ARCSSTE-E

The African Regional Centre for Space Science and Technology Education in English (ARCSSTE-E), Ile-Ife is a Government Public Parastatal under the supervision and control of the National Space Research and Development Agency (NARSDA), Abuja.
